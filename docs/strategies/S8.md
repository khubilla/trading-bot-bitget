## 2. When to Enter

The play: an S2-style breakout happened recently but price **didn't continue
upward** — it pulled back to a zone where three supports cluster. A small green
candle resting on that zone arms a stop-buy above its high.

### Step 1 — Post-S2 structure (single backward pass, `_find_structure`)

Find the most recent **breakout day B** within `S8_PHASE_LOOKBACK` completed
daily candles such that, as of day B:

- Daily RSI(14) on B > `S8_RSI_THRESH`
- A **big momentum candle** (body ≥ 20%) exists within 30 days before B
- A **tight Darvas coil** (1–5 candles, smallest window first) sits immediately
  before B — same effective-top/body-bottom math as S2 (GENERAL_CONCEPTS.md §1),
  range ≤ 15%, **all coil closes at/below the big candle's body top** (S2's
  containment rule — this stops impulse-continuation candles from registering
  as B)
- B's close is above the coil's effective box top

Outputs: `box_top` (Darvas effective coil high — support #1), `box_low`
(coil body-bottom low — fib leg anchor), RSI at B. The forming candle
(`iloc[-1]`) is never B.

### Step 2 — Impulse leg

`swing_high` = highest high from B through the last completed candle. Require
`swing_high > box_top × (1 + S8_MIN_EXTENSION)` — a real leg up, so the
retracement is meaningful.

### Step 3 — Tri-confluence zone

Three support levels must cluster within `S8_CONFLUENCE_TOL`:

1. `box_top` — the pre-breakout Darvas coil top (prior resistance → support)
2. `ma` — daily 20MA (SMA default; computed on completed candles)
3. `fib` — `swing_high − 0.618 × (swing_high − box_low)`

Zone = [min, max] of the three. Spread measured as `(max − min) / max`.

### Step 4 — Small green candle sitting on the zone

The **last completed** daily candle must be:
- Green (close > open)
- Small (body ≤ `S8_SMALL_BODY_PCT` of open)
- Sitting on the zone: `zone_low ≤ low ≤ zone_high × (1 + S8_PROXIMITY)`

### Step 5 — Entry trigger (pending watcher)

- `entry_trigger = green_high × (1 + S8_BREAKOUT_BUFFER)`
- Signal queued on `bot.pending_signals` (S2-style watcher, expires in 24h)
- Fires when `trigger ≤ mark ≤ trigger × (1 + S8_MAX_ENTRY_BUFFER)`
- Invalidated when mark < zone_low, or the s8 signal disappears on rescan
- Sentiment gate: evaluated only while market sentiment is **BULLISH**

### Sizing

Single **full-size** entry (`S8_TRADE_SIZE_PCT`, no scale-in — S2's scale-in is
entry-side machinery; S8 copies only the exits).

## 3. When to Exit (copies S2)

### Stop Loss
- `SL = max(green_low × 0.999, fill × (1 − S8_STOP_LOSS_PCT))`
- Computed at fire time from mark, attached to the entry market order as
  `presetStopLossPrice` — position is protected from the moment it opens
- The 5% cap prevents a deep green-candle low from exceeding the intended max loss

### Partial TP (50%)
- 50% closed at `fill × (1 + S8_TRAILING_TRIGGER_PCT)` via `profit_plan`

### Trailing Stop on Remaining 50%
- Exchange-side `moving_plan`, 10% callback, same trigger level
- Placement reuses `strategies/s2.py:_place_partial_trail_exits` (same
  cross-strategy reuse precedent as S7 → s4)

### Bot-Side Swing Trail (post-partial, if `S8_USE_SWING_TRAIL = True`)
Same reference-high-gated cycle as S2, on daily candles, active only after the
partial TP fires. SL never moves against the trade.

## 6. Active Trade Behavior (per tick)

While an S8 trade is open:

1. **PnL sync**: `unrealised_pnl` updated from exchange.
2. **Partial TP detection**: generic qty-drop detection logs `S8_PARTIAL`.
3. **Swing trail** (if enabled and partial fired): SL stepped to daily swing low.
4. **Close detection**: position gone → `S8_CLOSE` logged, trade removed.

No scale-in step — `scale_in_pending` is never set by `_fire_s8`.

## 8. Trade History on Exit

On close, `S8_CLOSE` appended to `trades.csv`. Win/loss determined by the
close-portion PnL; partial PnL logged separately (same convention as S2).

Pair pause rule applies: 3 losses in one UTC day → pair paused until midnight.
Strategy cooldown applies: 4h cooldown per symbol after a LOSS.
