## 2. When to Enter

The play: a big momentum move happened recently but price **didn't continue
upward** тАФ it pulled back to a zone where three supports cluster. A small green
candle resting on that zone arms a stop-buy above its high.

### Step 1 тАФ Post-S2 structure (single backward pass, `_find_structure`)

Find the most recent **big momentum candle B** ("the flight") within
`S8_PHASE_LOOKBACK` completed daily candles such that, as of candle B:

- B's body тЙе `S8_BIG_CANDLE_BODY_PCT` (default 20%)
- Daily RSI(14) on B > `S8_RSI_THRESH`
- The `S8_BASE_LOOKBACK` candles immediately **before** B form the **pre-flight
  base**: `box_top` = highest high of that base, `box_low` = lowest low. The
  base may coil but a **tight Darvas coil is NOT required** тАФ there is no
  tightness/range constraint on the base.
- B closes **above** `box_top` (it flew up and out of the base)

Outputs: `box_top` (pre-flight base high тАФ support #1, the breakout level price
retests), `box_low` (pre-flight base low тАФ fib leg anchor), RSI at B. The
forming candle (`iloc[-1]`) is never B.

### Step 2 тАФ Impulse leg

`swing_high` = highest high from B through the last completed candle. Require
`swing_high > box_top ├Ч (1 + S8_MIN_EXTENSION)` тАФ a real leg up, so the
retracement is meaningful.

### Step 3 тАФ Tri-confluence zone

Three support levels must cluster within `S8_CONFLUENCE_TOL`:

1. `box_top` тАФ the pre-flight base high (prior resistance тЖТ support)
2. `ma` тАФ daily 20MA (SMA default; computed on completed candles)
3. `fib` тАФ `swing_high тИТ 0.618 ├Ч (swing_high тИТ box_low)`

Zone = [min, max] of the three. Spread measured as `(max тИТ min) / max`.

### Step 4 тАФ Small green candle sitting on the zone

The **last completed** daily candle must be:
- Green (close > open)
- Small (body тЙд `S8_SMALL_BODY_PCT` of open)
- Sitting on the zone: `zone_low тЙд low тЙд zone_high ├Ч (1 + S8_PROXIMITY)`

### Step 5 тАФ Entry trigger (pending watcher)

- `entry_trigger = green_high ├Ч (1 + S8_BREAKOUT_BUFFER)`
- Signal queued on `bot.pending_signals` (S2-style watcher, expires in 24h)
- Fires when `trigger тЙд mark тЙд trigger ├Ч (1 + S8_MAX_ENTRY_BUFFER)`
- Invalidated when mark < zone_low, or the s8 signal disappears on rescan
- Sentiment gate: evaluated only while market sentiment is **BULLISH**

### Sizing

Single **full-size** entry (`S8_TRADE_SIZE_PCT`, no scale-in тАФ S2's scale-in is
entry-side machinery; S8 copies only the exits).

## 3. When to Exit (copies S2)

### Stop Loss
- `SL = max(green_low ├Ч 0.999, fill ├Ч (1 тИТ S8_STOP_LOSS_PCT))`
- Computed at fire time from mark, attached to the entry market order as
  `presetStopLossPrice` тАФ position is protected from the moment it opens
- The 5% cap prevents a deep green-candle low from exceeding the intended max loss

### Partial TP (50%)
- 50% closed at `fill ├Ч (1 + S8_TRAILING_TRIGGER_PCT)` via `profit_plan`

### Trailing Stop on Remaining 50%
- Exchange-side `moving_plan`, 10% callback, same trigger level
- Placement reuses `strategies/s2.py:_place_partial_trail_exits` (same
  cross-strategy reuse precedent as S7 тЖТ s4)

### Bot-Side Swing Trail (post-partial, if `S8_USE_SWING_TRAIL = True`)
Same reference-high-gated cycle as S2, on daily candles, active only after the
partial TP fires. SL never moves against the trade.

## 6. Active Trade Behavior (per tick)

While an S8 trade is open:

1. **PnL sync**: `unrealised_pnl` updated from exchange.
2. **Partial TP detection**: generic qty-drop detection logs `S8_PARTIAL`.
3. **Swing trail** (if enabled and partial fired): SL stepped to daily swing low.
4. **Close detection**: position gone тЖТ `S8_CLOSE` logged, trade removed.

No scale-in step тАФ `scale_in_pending` is never set by `_fire_s8`.
