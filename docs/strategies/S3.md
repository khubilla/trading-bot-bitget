## 2. When to Enter

### All conditions must pass (all on 15m unless noted):

**Step 1 — Daily Momentum Gate**
- Current 15m close must be ≥ 10% above today's daily open
- Ensures S3 only fires on strong intraday momentum days, not sideways days

**Step 2 — 15m EMA Golden Alignment**
- EMA10 > EMA20 > EMA50 > EMA200 (all on 15m)

**Step 3 — 15m ADX ≥ 30**
- Strong trend, not ranging

**Step 4 — Slow Stochastics Oversold**
- Slow Stochastics (5-period %K, 3-period smoothing) went below 30 within the last 8 completed 15m candles
- This confirms a pullback has occurred

**Step 5 — Green Uptick Candle**
- The **immediately completed 15m candle** (iloc[-2]) is green (close > open)
- The uptick must be the **direct next candle** after the last oversold bar — it cannot be more than one candle away from the current forming candle
- Entry trigger = uptick candle's high × (1 + 0.1%)

**Step 6 — Current Price Above Entry Trigger**
- Current 15m close must be above entry trigger
- If price is already > 1% above trigger, the entry is skipped (missed move)

**Step 7 — R:R ≥ 2.0**
- Risk = entry − SL (SL = pivot low of the oversold period × (1 − 0.2%))
- Reward = 10% of entry (partial TP level)
- R:R = reward / risk ≥ 2.0

**Step 8 — MACD Bullish**
- MACD line > signal line (momentum turning up)

**S/R Clearance Gate** (applied at execution):
- Find the highest high in last 50 15m candles (the pre-pullback peak) + 1%
- Search for the nearest 15m resistance above that level (300-candle lookback)
- If resistance is < 15% above current price, skip the trade

### Scale-in
S3 does **not** use scale-in. Full size at entry.

## 3. When to Exit

### Stop Loss
- `SL = pivot_low × (1 - 0.2%)` where `pivot_low` is the minimum low across all candles in the oversold period
- Placed as position-level SL on Bitget

### Partial TP (50%)
- 50% closed when price reaches entry + 10% (`S3_TRAILING_TRIGGER_PCT`)
- Placed as `profit_plan` order at entry time

### Trailing Stop on Remaining 50%
- Exchange-side `moving_plan` with 10% callback
- Activated at the same +10% trigger level

### Bot-Side Swing Trail (Reference-High Gated)

The swing trail uses a **reference swing high** as a gate. The SL only steps up after price has broken through a structural high, and only once a new swing low has formed **after** that broken high.

**Initialisation** (on trade open or bot restart):
- `swing_trail_ref` = nearest 15m swing high above current price (`find_swing_high_target`)

**Each tick:**
1. If `mark_price < swing_trail_ref` → do nothing, wait for price to reach the reference high
2. If `mark_price >= swing_trail_ref` → look for the first swing low that formed **after** the reference high candle (`find_swing_low_after_ref`)
   - Swing low found → `SL = swing_low × (1 − S3_SL_BUFFER_PCT)`; only apply if new SL is **higher** than current SL
   - Reset `swing_trail_ref` to the next swing high above current price → cycle repeats
   - No swing low found yet → do nothing, wait

**Key rule:** the SL does not move at the moment price breaks the reference high. It waits for a new swing low to form **after** that high. That low becomes the new SL, and the next structural high becomes the new reference.

**In-memory state:** `ap["swing_trail_ref"]` stores the current reference high price across ticks. Not persisted to state.json or CSV — resets cleanly to `None` on bot restart and reinitialises on the first tick.

## 6. Active Trade Behavior (per tick)

While an S3 trade is open:

1. **PnL sync**: `unrealised_pnl` updated from exchange.
2. **Partial TP detection**: if live qty drops below 75% of initial, `S3_PARTIAL` logged.
3. **Swing trail** (`S3_USE_SWING_TRAIL = True`): reference-high gated — SL only steps up after price breaks a structural swing high AND a new swing low forms after it. See §3 for full cycle description.
4. **Close detection**: when position disappears, `S3_CLOSE` logged.
